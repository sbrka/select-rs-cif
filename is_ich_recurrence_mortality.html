Clinical Time Zones and Risk Categories — Figures 2 & 4

Clinical time zones are monotone. IS: acute days 0–19, intermediate days 20–89, remote day 90+. ICH: acute days 0–4, intermediate days 5–20, remote day 21+. Once the remote symptomatic threshold is reached, the clinical time zone does not move back to intermediate.

Risk categories are instantaneous. Low = CIF <31.6%; medium = CIF 31.6–60%; high = CIF ≥60%. IS is low days 0–19, medium days 20–89 and ≥533, high days 90–532. ICH is medium day 0 and days 5–20, low days 1–4, high day 21+.
